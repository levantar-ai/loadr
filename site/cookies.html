Cookies on loadr.io

This site uses as few cookies as possible. We don't use any advertising or cross-site tracking cookies — only anonymous analytics, and only if you accept.

The short version

  • ▸ No cookies are set for analytics until you choose Accept on the cookie banner.
  • ▸ If you choose Decline, no analytics cookies are set and Google Analytics runs in a cookieless mode.
  • ▸ The loadr tool itself ships zero telemetry — it never phones home. This page is only about the website.

What we set, and only after you accept

Cookie Purpose Expires
_gaGoogle Analytics — tells returning visits apart, anonymously (IP anonymisation is on).2 years
_ga_<id>Google Analytics — keeps session state for the property.2 years

We also store your cookie choice itself in your browser's localStorage (key loadr.consent) so we don't ask again. That's a preference, not a tracking cookie, and never leaves your device.

Changing your choice

To change or withdraw consent at any time, clear this site's data in your browser (Site settings → Clear cookies and site data). The banner will then ask again on your next visit. You can also block analytics entirely with your browser's privacy settings or an extension.
